Hi! I'm Bianca and I would love to tell you a bit about my self and this project ... I was borned and raised in Argentina. I'm a professional cook and I've been doing that for the past 10 years. On 2016 I opened my own restaurant. It was such a great experience building that project, but two years later it had to come to an end.

Migrating to a new country looking for new opportunities

Leaving my restaurant was devastating and I had no idea what to do next. So I decided to join a friend on her trip to Barcelona. We decided to let things flow and see what happened.

I got a job at this great cultural cafe as a cook. I made friends, I learned to speak Catalan, I travelled a lot. And then the virus came. That's when I tripped over my reality. I knew I had to change careers, because I was already very tired of working as a cook and I wasn't enjoying it anymore. To be honest I never imagined I would get sick of my profession. But it happened.

New beginnings

I started an active search of what would be my new career. I tried many things, did many courses, went to many schools and talked to a lot of people. But the only thing that really caught my attention, was coding. My boyfriend lightly suggested it and I'm so greatfull for that, because I can't believe how much I'm enjoying it!

I've been studying and doing courses for the past three months and it's amazing how much I've learned so far and how hungry I am for more.

Allow me to finish this presentation about me just explaining very quickly something about this web. I see this project as a 'goodbye' to cooking professionaly and as a 'hello' to coding. I'll try to leave all my recipes here. Recipes I love and I've made so many times. Some of them are from my grandma, some others from my mom and the rest of them are experiments, restaurant anecdotes, friends sharing. I just hope you can enjoy them as much as I do!
